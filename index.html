SALT

What is SALT?

It's a recursive (self referring) acronym that stands for Salt and Light Technology. (SALT : SALT and Light Technology : SALT and Light Technology and Light Technology) Beyond that, I don't really know yet. It could be a company, a consulting firm, or even a school. The inspiration for the name came from Matthew 5:13-16, that this would be the salt and light to the tech industry. All I know is that it will be marked by a high degree of excellence.
